# Privacy Policy — Sidebed Light

_Last updated: 2026-06-19_

Sidebed Light ("the app") is a motion-activated bedside night light for Android. The
privacy summary is short: **the app collects nothing.**

## Data we collect
**None.** The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or device
data. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no accounts, and makes **no network
connections** of any kind.

## Device features the app uses (on-device only)
- **Accelerometer (motion sensor):** read on your device to detect movement and drive the
  light. Sensor readings never leave the device and are not recorded.
- **Camera flash (torch):** switched on/off as the light. The app does not access the
  camera, take photos, or record video (controlling the flash needs no camera permission).
- **Notification:** a single "Sidebed light is active" notification while the light is armed.
- **Display over other apps (optional):** only to draw the red-screen light, if you enable it.
- **Alarms &amp; reminders (optional):** only to run the nightly on/off schedule, if you enable it.
- **Wake lock:** to keep sensing while the screen is off.

Everything runs entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

## Permissions
The app requests only what those features require — foreground service, post
notifications, schedule exact alarms, system overlay, wake lock, and flashlight. It does
**not** request access to your location, contacts, microphone, storage, photos, or any
account.

## Children
The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone.

## Changes
Any future change to this policy will be posted in the app's GitHub repository.
